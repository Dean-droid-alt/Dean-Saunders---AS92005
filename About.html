About Earth's Future

Who We are

We are a group of people that care deeply about the planet. Our team consists of a group of scientists, researchers, environmental consultants, who want the best for us.



Our Aim

We aim to show values of Kaitiakitanga and inform people about climate change. Kaitiakitanga means guardianship and protection. It is a way of managing the environment, based on the Māori world view. In the Māori world view, people are closely connected to the land and nature. As Kaitiakitanga is based on the idea of humans being a part of the natural world, we hope to inspire people to take action, and fight against climate change.



Our motto

Our motto comes from the whakataukī or proverb. "Toitu te marae a Tane, Toitu te marae a Tangaroa, Toitu te iwi." Which translates to "If the land is well and the sea is well, the people will thrive."
